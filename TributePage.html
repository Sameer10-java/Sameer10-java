Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India • Scientist • Visionary President

About Him

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India (2002–2007). Revered as the "Missile Man of India" for his work in the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology, he is remembered for his humble lifestyle, dedication to students, and deep love for the country.

Why I Admire Him

What I deeply admire about Dr. Kalam is his commitment to knowledge and education. He rose from a humble background, inspired millions with his words and actions, and never stopped learning. His vision for a developed India continues to guide and inspire youth across the nation.

Famous Quotes

  • "Dream is not that which you see while sleeping, it is something that does not let you sleep."
  • "If you want to shine like a sun, first burn like a sun."
  • "You have to dream before your dreams can come true."

Legacy

Even after his passing in 2015, Dr. Kalam’ s ideas and teachings live on through his books, speeches, and initiatives. He remains a symbol of hope, knowledge, and the power of dreams.
